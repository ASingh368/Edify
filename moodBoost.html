MOOD BOOSTER ACTIVITIES

We all have days where we don’t feel good about ourselves. This is perfectly normal. If you have days were you feel more down than up, it can really boost your mood to volunteer or help someone in need. For example, you can, check for permission first, do something nice for someone. Perhaps help open a door for an elderly person or help at an animal shelter to help animals. There are many things you can do to make a difference! It’s has been shown that the more different the helping or volunteering activity is for you – the better!

*Please ask permission before volunteering and also ask the person you are helping. For example, many people who are disabled do not want to be helped unless they ask.
